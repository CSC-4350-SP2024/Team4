What is the service?

At CareerSwipe, we harness the power of artificial intelligence to transform the recruitment process. Our cutting-edge platform seamlessly extracts and analyzes details from resumes, offering a streamlined match-making experience that prioritizes precision and efficiency. Ideal for both students and recruiters, our service ensures that every connection is a step towards a successful future.

Why should I use it?

In the fast-paced world we live in, time is a precious commodity. Our platform stands out by offering:

  1. Speed: Say goodbye to lengthy screening processes. Our AI quickly identifies the most qualified candidates.
  2. Accuracy: Advanced machine learning tools ensure matches are based on precise criteria, from GPA to specific skill sets.
  3. Opportunity: For students, we provide a gateway to being discovered based on your true potential. For employers, access a pool of candidates ready to excel in their roles.

How can it help me?

Our platform is designed to streamline the recruitment process for both students and employers.

  1. For Students: Sign up, upload your resume, and answer a few questions. Our AI will make your profile visible to top recruiters based on your qualifications and preferences.
  2. For Employers: Define your ideal candidate criteria using our intuitive filters. Whether you seek specific skills, experiences, or academic achievements, we have you covered.
  3. Connect: Our system curates a list of candidates that perfectly match your criteria. Review their detailed profiles and connect with your future team members with just a click.
